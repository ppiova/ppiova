## About Me

I lead the AI Center of Excellence at OZ Digital Consulting, where I help drive the adoption of AI best practices, frameworks, and advanced capabilities across the organization.

My work is centered on designing and delivering practical AI solutions that improve efficiency, decision-making, and customer experience, while aligning technology with long-term business growth.

I am a Microsoft MVP in AI, recognized for contributing to the community through technical content, webinars, publications, demos, and knowledge sharing on Microsoft Foundry, Azure AI, and Generative AI.

I am also the author of [Ultimate Microsoft Certified Azure AI Engineer Associate (AI-102) Certification Guide](https://a.co/d/0hJv775S), a practical book for engineers and developers preparing for the AI-102 certification and building real-world solutions with Azure AI Services, Generative AI, and Microsoft Foundry.
